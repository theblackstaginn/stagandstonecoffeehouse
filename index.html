Built by Hand

Stag & Stone Coffeehouse is a handcrafted mobile coffee cart serving North Georgia markets, festivals, weddings, and private gatherings.

Espresso, pour overs, house teas, and fresh pastries served from a traveling old-world coffeehouse built for atmosphere as much as flavor.

What We Serve

Espresso drinks, iced coffee, pour overs, hot coffee, seasonal specials, house teas, and commissary-made pastries.

Made for Gatherings

Designed for farmers markets, evening events, renaissance fairs, weddings, breweries, and private celebrations.

Warm light, quiet atmosphere, and handcrafted coffee wherever the cart arrives.
